WHY TRANSLATION?

Sales:

Studies show that the majority of us tend to buy only if the product is presented in our own language. Local presence increases google rankings and conversion rates. In other words, there is money to be had from translation.

Confidence:

Studies show that the majority of us have more confidence in a text written in our own language. If you want to create trust, translation can help you on your way.

Understanding:

Translation ensures that the recipient understands the text and avoids any misunderstandings. Yes, it’s always nice to be understood correctly.

Requirements:

There may be requirements for product specifications, manuals, warnings and legal documents to be translated. In these cases, translation is a necessity.

PRODUCTS

Machine translation: Free

If you have large amounts of text that needs to be understood but not published, machine translation is a good solution, because it’s free and takes just a few minutes.

You can upload most document types or type in your text. A few minutes later we will send you an e-mail with your translation, translated by our own machines.

The fact that we use our own machines makes it safe for you to upload confidential material.

Basic:

Select Basic if you want to translate texts of low complexity. These may be short product texts, software, simple manuals, internal communication, newsletters and updates on social media.

Business:

Select Business if you want time spent on rewording adapted to the relevant language, so the translation will be fluent and the language natural.

You should also select Business when you have specialized text that requires industry knowledge, such as texts dealing with production, technology, transport, building construction, architecture, IT, medicine, cosmetics, health, nutrition, legal texts and professional articles.
